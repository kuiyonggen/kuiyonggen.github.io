Vacancies

Fulltime jobs, part-time jobs and internship are all accepted.

New Media Specialist

Job responsibilities:

  1. Focus on the observation and research of the Internet content industry, and be responsible for the topic selection, planning and writing of original articles.
  2. Assist in completing other work such as content publishing and operation.

Job requirements:

  1. Recognize the mission and values of the enterprise.
  2. Love the new media content industry and have the curiosity to continue to study and learn new things.
  3. Have a certain understanding of Internet hot events and industry development trends.
  4. High-quality writing skills, fluent communication and expression ability, empathy ability, able to complete creation independently and accurately.
  5. Media/new media-related experience is preferred, familiarity with various platforms, works, excellent English ability, video editing ability, etc. are preferred.

iOS Developer

Job responsibilities:

  1. Responsible for the research and development of mobile products and writing high-quality code.
  2. Have good communication and abstraction ability to cooperate with product managers, participate in product requirements discussions, functional definitions, etc.
  3. A well-designed code structure, continuous iterative reconstruction, and undertake key and difficult technical problems.

Job requirements:

  1. Strong thirst for knowledge and enterprising spirit, strong sense of responsibility and learning ability
  2. Have a solid programming background, good design ability and programming habits.
  3. Proficient in Swift, able to develop apps independently, excellent English ability, etc. are preferred.
