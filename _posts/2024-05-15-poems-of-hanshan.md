<pre>
 Someone exploring cold mountain way,
 
 Cold mountain is no through. 

- Han Shan
</pre>


<pre>
Someone laughs at my poetry,

My poetry for a dummy.

Dont't bother Confucius clarifying,

Why asking Laozi to teach?

No regret for no friends,

Just want a soul mate.

While the music is around,

I am falling into it.

Running into the right person,

It becomes a good journey.

- Han Shan
</pre>



All are my own translation from Chinese to English.
