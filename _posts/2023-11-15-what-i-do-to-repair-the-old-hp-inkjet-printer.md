Today, I decided to make the old hp inkjet printer work again at home. The printer hasn't worked for a couple of years. Luckily I made it and the steps are as follow.

1. I searched the printer's problem on the Internet and found that it could cause the printer to print blank pages after it was sleeping for a long time. Because the printing head or the ink box could be blocked by something. Luckily I found a solution on the Internet.

2. I placed the ink box into the hot water and be careful the ink out side must be downside with the box's half height in the water. Keep it for half to a hour.

3. Pick the ink box out, shake and swing it up and down to let ink out of it. Then make it dry and clean with a tissue.

4. Finally place it back into the printer and print a page. It works.

I don't know what makes the inkjet printer works because I don't take it apart to study it. But I know the most important parts are the printer's printing head and the ink box's ink out side part.

As the problem caused by the no use of a long time, it's better to use it at least once a week. If no time, can print via the internet, keeping the printer connected with the network.
