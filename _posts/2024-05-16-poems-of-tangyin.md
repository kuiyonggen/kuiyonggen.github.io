<pre>
The Peach Blossom Cottage in the Peach Blossom Castle,

The Peach Blossom Immortal in the Peach Blossom Cottage.

The Peach Blossom Immortal plants peach trees,

Then picks peach blossoms for wine money.

Just seat in front of peach blossoms while not drunk,

Come back under peach blossoms to sleep while drunk.

Only want old death between the blossoms and wine,

Never take a bow before carriages and horses.

Car dust and horse feet are nobles's fun,

Wine cups and flow branches are the poor's fate.

If compare the rich to the poor,

One is on earth and one is in the sky.

If compare poverty to carriages and horses,

He gets driving and I get free.

People laugh at me for too crazy,

I laugh at others who can't see through it.

- Tang Yin
